Senior Quality Assurance Engineer – Remote

Location: Russian speaking except Russia and Ukraine

Your Role:

You will have a key role in our fast-paced organisation. Our ideal candidate will be responsible for conducting tests before product launches to ensure software runs smoothly and meets client needs. You will be responsible for reviewing quality specifications and technical design documents to provide timely and meaningful feedback. You will create detailed, comprehensive and well-structured test plans and test cases. You will be responsible for estimating, prioritising, planning and coordinating quality testing activities including designing and implementing tests, debugging and defining corrective actions. You will also review system requirements and track quality assurance metrics.

  • Get things done while having fun

Responsibilities:

  • Review requirements, specifications and technical design documents to provide timely and meaningful feedback
  • Create detailed, comprehensive and well-structured test plans and test cases

About New Silver:

New Silver is a fintech startup that sits at the intersection of Decentralized Finance and real world assets. We build technology to originate and underwrite short term bridge loans with more efficiency, and securitize those loans on the blockchain with capital from DeFi protocols, helping to reduce costs and make securitizations more efficient. We are the first real world asset in the world to be approved by MakerDAO, a top-3 stablecoin protocol. Our small and rapidly growing company is globally located and fully remote. New Silver’s mission is to enable entrepreneurial growth by providing real estate investors with fast, conveniently accessible capital, and by harnessing technology to improve investors' journey, from property search to sale.
